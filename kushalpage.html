Big Project 1

Confusion. This art piece was made as a book cover, it explains a story of a teen struggling with depression and anxiety. The name of the book is confusion and it is the feeling that it portrayed by the teen throughout the enitre book except the end. In the end the teen finds a solution to his problems, which is music, thus the cover of the book cover is a man in headphones. The backgorund is a structure that is pictured to give a depth to the book cover, this depth is represented in the book as to how deep the teens problems are. The repeated pictures of the person brings out the emphasis on scatterdness and a "all over the place" type of vibe. I have received good feedback from this by making the layers of the teen spaced out a bit more so its not so hard on the eyes.
