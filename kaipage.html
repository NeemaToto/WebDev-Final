Career as a driver

When I was in Japan, I was crazy about drifting.
Drifting is a driving style that originated in Japan and gained attention in the racing scene around the 1990s.
I started drifting in 2020 under the influence of my initial d.
Every night after work, I would always practice drifting at a race track or mountain pass.
I also acquired a racing license and often participated in amateur races. I owned two cars, a Toyota 86 and a Nissan Fairlady Z.
These two cars trained me as a race driver, and the concentration and decision-making skills I developed during that time are still useful today as a developer.
